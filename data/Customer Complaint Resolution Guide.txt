Customer Complaint Resolution Guidelines

1. Delayed Delivery
Customers experiencing delayed deliveries should contact support with their order number. Delivery issues are generally resolved within 48 hours.

2. Damaged Products
Customers may request a replacement or refund for damaged products within seven days of delivery. Photographic evidence may be required.

3. Incorrect Items
If customers receive incorrect items, they should report the issue within five days. Replacement shipments are prioritized.

4. Refund Processing
Approved refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

5. Escalation Policy
Unresolved complaints are escalated to senior support specialists after 72 hours.

6. Order Cancellation
Orders can be canceled before shipment. Once shipped, cancellation requests are processed as returns.

7. Missing Packages
Customers should report missing packages within 10 days of the expected delivery date.
