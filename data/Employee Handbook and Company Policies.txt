Employee Handbook and Company Policies

1. Working Hours
Employees are expected to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible work arrangements may be approved by managers.

2. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave annually. Sick leave can be availed with proper documentation.

3. Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to three days a week with manager approval.

4. Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professionalism and respect in all interactions. Harassment or discrimination will not be tolerated.

5. Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted every six months. Promotions and bonuses are based on performance and business requirements.

6. Expense Reimbursement
Business-related expenses must be submitted within 30 days with supporting receipts.

7. Confidentiality
Employees are prohibited from sharing confidential company information with external parties.

8. IT Usage Policy
Company laptops and email accounts should be used primarily for work-related activities. Unauthorized software installation is prohibited.
