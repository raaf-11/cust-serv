Customer Account and Security Help

1. Password Reset
Customers can reset their password by selecting "Forgot Password" on the login page. A password reset link will be sent to their registered email address.

2. Changing Password
Customers can change their password from Account Settings under the Security section.

3. Updating Email Address
Customers can update their email address from Profile Settings after verifying their identity.

4. Two-Factor Authentication
Customers can enable two-factor authentication for additional account security.

5. Locked Account
Accounts are temporarily locked after five unsuccessful login attempts. Access is automatically restored after 30 minutes.

6. Username Recovery
Customers who forget their username can retrieve it using their registered email address.

7. Session Management
Customers can view and log out of active sessions from the Security Settings page.

8. Deleting an Account
Customers may request account deletion through the Privacy Settings section. Account deletion requests are processed within 14 days.
